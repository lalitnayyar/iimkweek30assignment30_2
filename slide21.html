Continuous Monitoring - MLOps and Model Governance

MLOps governance frameworks integrate automated processes for tracking, validation, and documentation across the AI lifecycle

Machine Learning Operations (MLOps) governance provides the operational foundation for continuous AI monitoring and compliance. **Automated tracking and monitoring** captures comprehensive metadata about model development, training data, feature engineering, hyperparameters, and performance metrics, creating complete lineage documentation. **Validation processes** ensure models meet quality, performance, and fairness standards before deployment, with automated testing that checks for regression, bias, and compliance violations.

**Versioning systems** maintain complete histories of all ML artifacts including datasets, code, models, and configurations, enabling reproducibility and rollback when issues emerge. **Reproducible pipelines** automate data processing, model training, and deployment workflows, reducing manual errors and ensuring consistency. **Model governance frameworks** establish policies for model approval, deployment authorization, ongoing monitoring requirements, and retirement criteria.

**Continuous integration/continuous deployment (CI/CD)** for ML enables rapid, safe updates while maintaining quality and compliance standards. **Collaboration tools** facilitate communication between data scientists, engineers, compliance officers, and business stakeholders, ensuring alignment on objectives and requirements. Effective MLOps governance bridges the gap between AI development and operational reliability.
