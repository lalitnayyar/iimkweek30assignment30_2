Compliance in Regulated Industries

Finance and healthcare sectors face unique compliance challenges requiring specialized AI governance approaches

Regulated industries operate under heightened scrutiny where AI failures can result in significant harm to individuals and substantial penalties for organizations. The **finance sector's** AI systems influence credit decisions, investment recommendations, fraud detection, and risk assessment—areas governed by anti-discrimination laws, consumer protection regulations, and financial stability requirements.

The **healthcare sector's** AI affects patient diagnosis, treatment recommendations, medical device functionality, and health information management—domains regulated by FDA medical device guidelines, HIPAA privacy requirements, and patient safety standards.

These sectors share common challenges: ensuring data privacy and security, maintaining transparency in high-stakes decisions, demonstrating compliance with sector-specific standards, and implementing continuous monitoring to detect and correct issues. However, they also face unique requirements that demand specialized governance approaches. The following slides examine these sector-specific considerations and establish comprehensive monitoring strategies.
